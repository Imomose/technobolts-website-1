ROBOT SHOWCASE

Meet "Doggo"

Meet "Doggo"— our 2025-2026 season competition robot, engineered for speed, precision, and reliability on the FTC playing field.

Turret System

Advanced turret mechanism for precise targeting and scoring with 360° rotation capability.

  • High-precision servo control
  • Rapid fire capability
  • Integrated vision tracking

Limelight Vision

Advanced computer vision system for autonomous target detection and alignment.

  • Real-time target tracking
  • Automatic alignment
  • Low-latency processing

Heck of an Auto

Sophisticated autonomous routine with complex pathfinding and scoring sequences.

  • Multi-stage scoring
  • Adaptive positioning
  • Fail-safe recovery

Technical Specifications

Drive System

Mecanum wheels for omnidirectional movement

Power

REV Robotics battery system with dual motors

Control

Android-based control system with Java programming

Sensors

Limelight, IMU, and encoder feedback systems
