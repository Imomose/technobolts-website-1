WHO WE ARE

About the Team

We are the Technobolts, a FIRST Tech Challenge team dedicated to robotics and aiming for the stars.

Our Mission

We believe that robotics is more than a competition — it's a platform for developing the future generation. Through hands-on design, programming, and collaboration, our team members gain real-world engineering skills that extend far beyond the playing field.

Founded in 2024, we have made massive improvements since our rookie year and keeping trying to do so. As a team of 8 members, we prioritize hard work, innovation, and teamwork.
